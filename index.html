The Student

I am a Junior at Prairie View Agricultural & Mechanical University studying Computer Science and Spanish. I am also a member of Prairie View's Honors Program.

I chose to study Computer Science because I wanted to be a game developer, but as I got older, I outgrew that dream and video games. Now, I study Computer Science because I believe that code can change the world. I want to use the skills I gain from Computer Science to solve educational, economical and social issues. As for Spanish, I’ve always been fascinated by the language and culture. I was forced to take Spanish in 7th grade and I loved it. I loved it so much that I studied it all throughout high school up until the highest class offered - AP Spanish 5. Now, I’m minoring to continue the process I started in middle school and further stand out from everyone else.

I like to think that I am an aspiring entrepreneur and philanthropist, but in reality, I have no clue what I want in life. I also like to think that I am going to pursue my Ph.D. right after receiving my B.S. from Prairie View, but God may have something different planned.

Stay tuned for updates.

FAQs & Booking

How will I receive my photos?

After the shoot, you will receive a link to all the unedited images. Using the link, you can download and choose which images you would like me to edit. Once your selected images are edited, you will receive another link. All links expires after 2 weeks, so download and backup everything.

Booking your session.

In order to secure your date and time, I require a 50% non-refundable deposit via Venmo or Cash App. Once the deposit has been paid, I will schedule a consultation date to properly prepare for the shoot. There is also a $25 late fee for arriving to the shoot more than 15 minutes late.

What if I want to reschedule?

If the weather is bad or I am too sick to shoot, you can put your deposit toward a reschedule or have it refunded. If you need to cancel or do not show up to the shoot, the deposit will not be refunded.
